Editing a VO

To edit the settings of a VO open the g-Eclipse > VO-Declarations preference page. Select the VO you would like to edit and click Edit.... Now the VO Wizard shows up and automatically switches to the wizard page where you may edit the settings of the selected VO. Note that you can change any setting except the VO's name. After performing your changes, click Finish and your new VO settings will be saved.

To start editing a VO you can also simply double-click the VO in the list of all currently available VOs.
